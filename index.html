About the VTC ATS

The Independent Owner-Operators (I.O.O.) VTC is a community for serious American Truck Simulator drivers focused on realism, camaraderie, and clean convoys. We run weekly events, maintain fair play standards, and support drivers with tools and guides to keep the roads fun.

How to Join

1) Click Join our Discord above.
2) Go to #applications and follow the pinned instructions.
3) A coordinator will review and get you rolling.

Events & Convoys

We schedule convoys weekly and post routes, start times, and DLC requirements in Discord. Check the #events channel for the latest info and photos from past runs.

Giveaways

100% of donations go to community giveaway items (gift cards, DLC, merch). Winners are announced in Discord after each event.

Contact

Questions about the VTC or partnerships? Jump into Discord or email us at info@ioovtc.com.
